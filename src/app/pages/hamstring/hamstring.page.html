Hamstring Strain
Symptoms
The quadricep and hamstring are located in the different parts of your leg; the quadricep being a group of four muscles in front of your thigh connecting to your kneecap and the hamstring being a group of three muscles that are along the back of your thigh.
Treatments
In cases of both injuries, activity is not recommended to continue if servere and a change of routine is advised. Whether that is more stretching or exercising before physical activity, more caution is to be placed in those specific areas.
