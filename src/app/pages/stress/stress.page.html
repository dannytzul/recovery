Stress Fracture
Symptoms
A stress fracture is a small crack in the surface of a bone, essentially affecting your overall body foundation. This will play restrictions in movement as a whole, as stress fractures often occur when there is an increase in the intensity and volume of our training.
Treatments
Since they are not your average injuries that can be treated by oneself, a proper x-ray or bone scan should be done to provide treatment and doctor recommendations. After aided by the doctor, one is not to apply any added-on pressure to the area in healing to prevent making the injury worse. Exercises targeting different parts of the body can be one however if desired.
