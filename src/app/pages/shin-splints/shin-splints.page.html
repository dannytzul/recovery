Shin Splits
Symptoms
Pain or discomfort that runs down the tibia (shin bone) that is caused due to constant intense activity, overworking muscles, tendons, and bone tissue.
Treatments
In order to treat this problem, follow these steps:
Rest
The injured area for 48 hours.
Steps
Ice
For 20 minutes at a time, 4 to 8 times per day.
Steps
Compress
To help reduce swelling.
Steps
Elevate
The injured limb 6 to 10 inches above the heart.
