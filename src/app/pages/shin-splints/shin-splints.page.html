Shin Splints
Symptoms
Pain or discomfort that runs down the tibia (shin bone) that is caused due to constant intense activity, overworking muscles, tendons, and bone tissue. Shin splints are gradually developed over time as the runner is either beginning to run great distances or an abrupt change in their former routine is p resented.
Treatments
Experts advise runners to either decrease the amount of activity done or to stop running entirely for awhile until the pain is at ease. When a return is possible, runners must remember to build up their training rather than going back to harsh activity right after.
